How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your Ampairs account and associated data directly from within the mobile app. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Open the Ampairs mobile app
  2. Go to Settings or Profile
  3. Tap on Account Settings
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm your deletion request
Important: Account deletion cannot be undone after 30 days. You have a 30-day grace period to cancel the deletion request if you change your mind.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • All authentication tokens and session data
  • Your workspace memberships (if not sole owner)
  • Personal preferences and settings
  • Firebase authentication data

Data Retention Period

After requesting account deletion:

  • 30-day grace period: Your account is marked for deletion but can be restored
  • After 30 days: Your account and all personal data are permanently deleted
  • Workspace data: If you're the sole owner of a workspace, you must transfer ownership or delete the workspace before deleting your account

Cancelling a Deletion Request

If you change your mind within the 30-day grace period, you can cancel the deletion request by:

  1. Logging back into the Ampairs app
  2. Going to Settings → Account Settings
  3. Selecting "Cancel Account Deletion"
